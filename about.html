Our Mission

Founded in 2026, Voiceover Directory started with a simple belief: finding the perfect voice shouldn't be hard. We built a platform that connects talented voice actors directly with clients, stripping away the complexities of traditional agencies.

Today, we are home to thousands of artists from over 100 countries, speaking 50+ languages. Whether you're an indie developer or a global brand, we're here to help you be heard.
